Relection

20 May 2016

  • What's your take on the DBC / EDA experience?
    From my experience of phase-0 so far, i feel very well supported and informed.
    The ciriculum feels well put together and organic. Waffle board is great for keeping organised.
  • What are your impressions?
    My cohort and coaches all seem to be very motivated and kind.
  • How do you see yourself engaging with this type of culture?
    I see myself fitting in very well with the like minded and supportive people I have met so far.
    I can't wait to start pair programming with them, andreally looking forward to getting stuck into some long intense days at DBC in Welli.
  • Have your expectations of EDA changed? If so, how?
    I have reaslied that I am going to need to dedicate alot more time during the week to the phase-0 assignment and learning.
    There is not enough time to get it all done over the weekends.
    Pomodoro technique is going to be my new best friend I think.
  • Are you excited to participate in this kind of learning environment? Does it make you nervous?
    Yes I am very excited about this learning environment. I have done the whole university thing,
    and I eventually learned what I needed to know, but it too way took long,
    and I feel that the EDA way of teaching and learning will be vastly more benificial for me.
    Yes it does make me nervous, I am worried about quitting my job and going back to full-time study.
    However I feel that EDA will give me the best shot at getting into a new field,
    that I have the potential to excel at, but most importantly, enjoy myself
